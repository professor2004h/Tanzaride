How TANZARIDE Works

Simple, transparent, and efficient car rental process

The TANZARIDE Process

Three simple steps to connect car owners with customers

Step 1: Car Owners Register Their Vehicles

Vehicle owners create an account and list their cars on the TANZARIDE platform. They provide essential details including car model, year, location, availability dates, and upload photos. Our team verifies each listing to ensure quality and authenticity.

Step 2: Customers Browse and Book

Travelers and locals search for available vehicles based on their location, dates, and preferences. They can compare different options, view detailed information, and book their chosen car instantly through our secure platform.

Step 3: TANZARIDE Takes a Small Commission

When a rental is completed successfully, TANZARIDE earns a small commission from the transaction. This commission-based model keeps our platform running and ensures we provide quality service to both car owners and renters.

Our Commission-Based Model

How We Earn

TANZARIDE operates on a transparent commission-based revenue model. We don't own any vehicles ourselves. Instead, we provide the platform, marketing, customer service, and payment processing that connects car owners with renters.

For each successful rental transaction, TANZARIDE takes a small percentage as commission. This fee covers:

  • Platform maintenance and development
  • Marketing and customer acquisition
  • Customer support services
  • Payment processing and security
  • Verification and quality assurance

This model ensures that we only succeed when our car owners and customers succeed. It aligns our interests with yours and motivates us to provide the best possible service.
